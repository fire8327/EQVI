EQVI Balance Awards™

A living loyalty program with a development currency — Libra

Libra is awarded for everyday activities:

  • Purchases in food and fashion categories
  • Workouts
  • Participation in quizzes
  • Collective achievements
  • Other activities within the EQVI ecosystem

When the body is in balance — sleep, activity, and calories are within normal range — the Libra level increases and bonuses are doubled.

Accumulated Libra can be converted into cashback and used for health, education, electronics, leisure, and entertainment — for yourself and your loved ones.

Family, friend, and team Libra accounts are available: rewards can be earned and spent together.
