Platform

EQVI is an AI-powered Life Operating System that enables banks to understand customers beyond transactions and engage them through personalized, everyday experiences.

EQVI operates directly inside your bank’s mobile application. The platform is built on six interconnected layers.

1. Vertical expert teams train and continuously refine AI models for each life domain — including nutrition, style, fitness, health, travel, family, and more.

2. A unified customer profile combines signals from every domain into a single, holistic understanding of each person.

3. Cross-domain intelligence connects insights across these domains to identify the customer’s next most relevant need.

4. A recommendation engine delivers personalized experiences in the right context, at exactly the right moment.

5. A continuous feedback loop captures user behavior and ratings to improve every recommendation.

Finally, a self-learning intelligence layer becomes measurably more accurate over time as it learns from millions of interactions.

As a result, the banking app evolves from a transactional interface into an intelligent everyday companion.
