Integration

Designed for fast integration into existing banking infrastructure — without replacing core systems or disrupting customer journeys.

Getting started with EQVI begins with a short technical briefing, allowing our integration team to understand your existing architecture, systems, and technical requirements.

Based on this, we prepare integration documentation tailored specifically to your infrastructure.

EQVI is built on REST APIs and SDKs designed for fast, low-friction integration with core banking platforms, CRM systems, loyalty platforms, payment infrastructure, and existing digital channels.

Deployment is fully modular. Banks can begin with a single domain — such as nutrition or style — validate business value through a focused pilot, and expand into additional domains whenever they’re ready.

There is no need to deploy the complete platform from day one, and no need to rebuild your existing infrastructure.

The entire experience remains inside your banking application, under your brand, while the customer relationship remains entirely owned by your bank.
